Simple Present

I work every day

Rotina, hábitos e fatos gerais

  • She studies math
  • They play soccer
  • It rains a lot here
Subject + V(s/es) + complement
Present Continuous

I am working now

Ações acontecendo agora mesmo

  • She is studying now
  • They are playing
  • It is raining
Subject + am/is/are + V-ing
Simple Past

I worked yesterday

Ações finalizadas no passado

  • She studied last night
  • They played yesterday
  • It rained all day
Subject + V(ed/irreg) + complement
Future (will)

I will work tomorrow

Ações e planos futuros

  • She will study later
  • They will play tonight
  • It will rain tomorrow
Subject + will + V (base form)
Modal Verbs

Modals in Tech Context

Comunicação profissional em inglês

  • can — The system can validate data.
  • should — Developers should document the API.
  • must — Users must authenticate first.
  • could — We could redesign the interface.
Subject + Modal + Base Verb (no "to", no -s/-ing)
Passive Voice

Passive Voice in Tech Context

Changelogs, bug reports e documentação

  • is/are + V3 — User data is encrypted.
  • was/were + V3 — The bug was fixed.
  • has been + V3 — A new version has been released.
  • will be + V3 — The update will be installed.
Subject + be (am/is/are/was/were/been) + Past Participle
